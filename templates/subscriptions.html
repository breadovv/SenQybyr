How do subscriptions work?

When you subscribe to a channel, you'll see their latest videos in your subscriptions feed. You'll also receive notifications about new uploads (if enabled).

Can I manage my subscriptions?

Yes! You can unsubscribe from any channel by clicking the "Unsubscribe" button on their channel tile. Your subscription list is always accessible from the sidebar.

Are subscriptions free?

Absolutely! All subscriptions on SenQubyr are completely free. You can subscribe to unlimited channels without any cost.

How do I find new channels to subscribe to?

Browse the Home, Trending, and Shorts pages to discover new content creators. Click on any video to visit the channel, then hit the Subscribe button.

Will I get notifications from subscribed channels?

You can customize your notification preferences in your account settings. Choose to receive alerts for all uploads, live streams, or only occasional updates.
