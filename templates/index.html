What is SenQubyr?

SenQubyr is a video sharing platform inspired by YouTube, created as part of a web development course at AITU. It features video browsing, subscriptions, and user profiles.

How do I create an account?

Click on the profile icon in the header, then select "Sign Up". Fill in your information including email, password, and preferred content types.

Can I upload videos?

Yes! Once you have an account, click the "+" icon in the header to upload your videos. Supported formats include MP4, AVI, and MOV.

How do subscriptions work?

Subscriptions allow you to follow your favorite channels. When you subscribe, you'll get notifications about new uploads.

Is SenQubyr free to use?

Absolutely! SenQubyr is completely free. You can watch unlimited videos, subscribe to channels, and upload your own content.
